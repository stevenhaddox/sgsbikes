About Us: Meet the founders

Cody Painter
Club President/Founder
I am the president and founder of Saint George Street Bikes. I am 23 years old and I am currently attending Dixie State University. I have lived in southern Utah since 2011. I love it out here. When I moved here I saw tons of people riding sports bikes and thought to myself it would be great to get everyone together, so I did! I have met some amazing guys and we are constantly growing! It's awesome! I ride a 2007 Zx6R and love it. I hope you enjoy the website!

Dennis Bee
Club President
Dennis Bee is the co-founder and one of the presidents of Saint George Street Bikes. He is from Las Vegas, NV and moved to Saint George, UT in 2011. He has been riding sport bikes since 2009 and currently owns a 2005 Kawasaki Ninja ZX6R (636). As president of the crew he plans out rides and events as well as recruits new riders.
