Upcoming Events

Lake Havasu Ride:

March 8th-10th Saint George Street Bikes is doing it big with a Spring Break ride to Lake Havasu AZ. We are heading out on March 8th and making a stop in Las Vegas to rest, fuel up, and get something to eat. After that we are heading down to Lake Havasu. We are planning on staying both nights in Havasu with hotel rates being as low as $55-65$ a night. (Hit us up if you would like to know what hotel we are looking at staying at) In Lake Havasu we are planning on doing some riding, relaxing, and partying it up SGSB style! March 10th we are departing Havasu and stopping again in Las Vegas to rest, fuel up, and get something to eat then heading back to Saint George. Don't miss out on this epic event! (Let us know if you plan on going so we can get a count and possibly a group rate at one of the hotels. Also if you do not own a bike, cars are more then welcome to come with us!)

Past Events

Saint George Street Bikes First Photo Shoot:

We held our first ever photo shoot last november. The turn out was great. We took pictures that show cased the club members bikes, we also had some girls come out and do some modeling with the bikes. Everyone had a great time. Go check out the gallery to see the pictures of it and keep an ear out for the next one!

First Charity Ride:

On Monday December 17th 2012 SGSB with Santa of course set off on a mission to put smiles on the faces of the students of a few local schools. We delivered toy motorcycles to special needs kids and let them sit on the real bikes and look at them. Please check out our gallery to see pictures of our ride. We hope to have many more charity rides in the future. Thanks to everyone that donated toys for the kids.

First Ever Ride:

In October of 2012 Saint George Street Bikes had there first ever ride as a club! It was definitely a monumental moment for the club. The Ruff Ryders from the the Salt Lake City Chapter and Las Vegas Chapter showed up to vest our very own Dennis Bee as a member with them. After the vesting we took off from Saint George and drove up to Zion National Park. We stopped up there and took a couple minutes to enjoy the beauty then rode back home. Check out the gallery for the pictures of the ride!
